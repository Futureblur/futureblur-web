About me

I love programming! In fact, I spend most of my time doing this, because it is so much fun. In the past few years, I have been actively developing 3D first-person games using Unity and Unreal Engine. Unfortunately, I have never finished any of these, but I have participated in the GMTK Game Jam in 2020 and proudly finished Cube Control.

While making games is fun, I was slowly realizing that I wasn't making much progress, so I decided to move on to new things. I started to learn C++ and shortly afterwards I felt strong enough to get started with OpenGL, a cross-platform API for rendering. Since then, I couldn't stop myself from diving deeper and deeper into the world of CGI and software engineering.

When I don't feel like programming or want to take a break, I open up blender and make some 3D renders. If I'm happy with the result, I share the artwork on social media.
